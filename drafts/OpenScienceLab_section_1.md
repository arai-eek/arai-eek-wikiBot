## Open Science Lab Users

The Open Science Lab (Hackteria ZET) is a transdisciplinary meeting point for a diverse community of hackers, artists, foodies, and fermentation activists. Current and recent collaborative research nodes include:

*   **Open Science Hardware**: Development of low-cost, open-source lab instruments (e.g., DIY microscopes, PCR thermocyclers).
*   **Biofabrication**: Experiments with growing garments and materials from bacteria and fungi.
*   **Technobiological Futures**: International knowledge exchange programme (CH, TH, ID, IN).
*   **Hacker-in-Residence**: Hosting international researchers such as the **XENOPIA** project (hormone testing) and the **Abao Nano Doctor Blade** residency.
*   **thGAP**: Collaborative research on gene editing and bio-art.
