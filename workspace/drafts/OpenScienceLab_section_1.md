## Open Science Lab Users

![Arai-eek Centipede Test 2](../media/arai-eek_centipede_test2.jpg)



The Open Science Lab (Hackteria ZET) is a transdisciplinary meeting point for a diverse community of hackers, artists, foodies, and fermentation activists. Current and recent collaborative research nodes include:

*   **[Open Science Hardware](Bio_Lab_Infrastructure)**: Development of low-cost, open-source lab instruments (e.g., DIY microscopes, PCR thermocyclers).
*   **[Biofabrication](Kitchenlab_Bioplastic)**: Experiments with growing garments and materials from bacteria and fungi.
*   **[Technobiological Futures](Technobiological_Futures_Co-Laboratories)**: International knowledge exchange programme (CH, TH, ID, IN).
*   **[Hacker-in-Residence](https://mechatronicart.ch/hacker-in-residence-programme/)**: Hosting international researchers such as the **[XENOPIA](Xenopia)** project (hormone testing) and the **[Abao Nano](Abao_Nano_Doctor_Blade_Hacker_Residency)** residency.
*   **[thGAP](Mind_thGAP)**: Collaborative research on gene editing and bio-art.
